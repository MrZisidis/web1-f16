Tasks: Final Project

  1. TODO List

    • Take a look at the TODO list you wrote last week and use it to prioritize your work. I find the following scheme works well with web projects: note the items on your list that are critical, and note the items that are easy (IE you know exactly how to make them in HTML & CSS). Then do the easy and critical items first.

    Example prioritized TODO list:
    1. critical/easy Add email address to contact page
    2. easy Fix Logo styling
    3. critical Purchase domain & upload site to yourname.com
    4. critical Add category page for all works
    5. Add page footer
    6. Finish adding individual work pages for paintings
    7. Cleanup iPad layout
    8. Use JS Lightbox when clicking on images
    9. Animate homepage images

  2. Content

    • Your content should be fully completed at this point, with the exception of individual work pages, which is often a never-ending processâ€¦

  3. CSS

    • Spend time on your CSS. Pay attention to font sizing, colors, background colors and textures, link colors, hover states, margins and padding.
    • Verify your site looks and behaves properly on multiple web browsers, platforms (including phones & tablets) and browsers sizes.
    • If you haven't already you should at this point have started adding a responsive web design breakpoint to fine-tune the mobile experience.
