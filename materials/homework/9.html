Goals

  1. Practice translating site needs into visual designs
  2. Practice building compelling visual mockups that promote your ideas

Deliverables

Due by the start of next class.

Personal Portfolio Site

Create additional hand-drawn sketches (or whatever else you desire) as needed for your own design process.
  1. Create several low-fidelity wireframes of various pages of your personal portfolio website to illustrate the general layout of components. Post as an image to your process page.
  2. Create a beautiful Style Tile for your personal portfolio website. Post as an image or image thumbnail with link to a PDF on your process page.

Revisions

The following are suggested incremental steps towards moving your projects forward. They do not have a fixed due date, manage your time appropriately.

  1. How does your Client Site look? Is it ready to receive feedback from your client? If so, meet with your client, take notes and post on your process page. Make a list of remaining tasks, or create issues on your GitHub page.
  2. Test your Student Page, Event Promotion and Visual Portfolio on various browsers and devices including phones, tablets, laptops and large screens. Add media query breakpoints as necessary to vary the design.
