Deliverables

Due by the start of next class.

Client Site

Create additional hand-drawn sketches (or whatever else you desire) as needed for your own design process.
  1. Create several low-fidelity wireframes of various pages of your client website to illustrate the general layout of components.
  2. Create a beautiful Style Tile for your client website. Post as an image or image thumbnail with link to a PDF on your client process page.
  3. Schedule a meeting with your client to present your wireframes and Style Tile for feedback. Document this meeting on your process page (this does not need to happen before the assignment due date).

Revisions

The following are suggested incremental steps towards moving your projects forward. They do not have a fixed due date, manage your time appropriately.

  1. Add CSS styles and layouts to your Student Page
  2. Revisit your Event Promotion and Visual Portfolio pages. Is there anything you would like to update with your new CSS skills?
